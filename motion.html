Wand Motion Magic

Use your hand as a wand to cast spells!

How to Play:

This game uses your camera to track hand movements.

Point your index finger to cast the Lumos spell.

Make sure you have a webcam and allow camera access when prompted.

The game works best in good lighting conditions.

Or press any key to start
