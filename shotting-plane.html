Quidditch Seeker

Fly your broomstick and defend against Bludgers!

How to Play:

Control your broomstick with arrow keys:

↑ Move Up

↓ Move Down

← Move Left

→ Move Right

Press SPACE to cast spells and destroy Bludgers.

Avoid letting Bludgers pass you!

Press any key to start the game...
